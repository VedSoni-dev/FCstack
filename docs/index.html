SKILL SUITE

Nine ways to think
like a YC partner.

Every skill reads your codebase. Every piece of advice is about YOUR company.

Analysis

review

Full startup analysis. Scores idea, market, product, moat, growth, team. Cites real YC essays with URLs.

Feedback

roast

PG-style brutal honesty. Strips your marketing language. Names the hard truth you won't say out loud.

Application

apply

Interactive application builder. All 17 questions, one at a time. Drafts from your codebase, stress-tests each answer.

Interview

interview

Mock 10-minute partner interview. Michael & Dalton grill you. Interruptions. Follow-ups. Scorecard with ACCEPT/REJECT verdict.

Pitch

demo-day

60-second Demo Day pitch from your codebase. Scores each section. Tests against three investor archetypes.

Coaching

office-hours

Pick a partner. Bring a problem. They ask the questions that lead you to the answer. Ends with homework.

Data

metrics

Hunts your codebase for growth signals. Analytics, payments, auth. Recommends your north star metric.

Readiness

speed-run

Full pre-flight check. 10 dimensions scored. Overall readiness verdict. Prioritized action plan for the week.

Research

founders-like-you

Finds YC alumni with similar companies. Surfaces their pitches, pivots, and growth stories. Learn from pattern matching.
